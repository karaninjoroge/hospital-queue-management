# Journey Map — Hospital Queue Management Platform

**Author:** Karani Njoroge
**Date:** June 2026
**Primary Persona:** Mama Jane (Patient)

---

## Current State Journey (Before Product)

| Stage | Jane's Action | Her Emotion | Pain Points |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Decision** | Decides she needs to see a doctor. Asks neighbors when is best to go. | Anxious, uncertain | No reliable information — folk wisdom varies |
| **Arrival** | Wakes at 4:30am. Travels to facility. Arrives 5:15am to secure early position. | Resigned, tired | Loses sleep and morning income just to get a good position |
| **Registration** | Writes her name in a paper ledger. Gets no confirmation or number. | Neutral | No proof of registration. No reference point for her position. |
| **Waiting** | Sits in crowded waiting room. Watches nurses call names. Has no idea of her position. | Frustrated, anxious | No updates. Cannot leave. Cannot estimate how long. |
| **Conflict Moment** | Another patient pushes in. Argument breaks out. Nurse intervenes. | Angry, helpless | No objective proof of order. Resolution is arbitrary. |
| **Decision Point** | At 11am, 5.5 hours waiting, she must open her stall. | Distressed | Has to choose between care and income |
| **Abandonment** | Leaves without being seen. Goes to chemist for medicine. | Defeated, worried | Health risk. Money wasted. System failed her. |

## Future State Journey (With Product)

| Stage | Jane's Action | Her Emotion | Product Touchpoint |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Decision** | Decides she needs to see a doctor. | Calm | — |
| **Pre-Registration** | Sends SMS to facility number. Receives queue number and estimated slot. | Relieved, in control | SMS registration system |

| **Seen** | Sees clinical officer. Gets prescription. | Satisfied | — |
| **Exit** | Leaves by 10am. Opens stall. Loses 3 hours, not a full day. | Positive | — |

## Nurse Mary — Current State Journey

| Stage | Mary's Action | Her Emotion | Pain Points |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Arrives 6:30am** | Crowd already formed. Arguments beginning. | Stressed | Starting the day in conflict |
| **Queue setup** | Organizes physical queue by memory and ledger. | Focused, tired | No objective system. Disputes immediate. |
| **Conflict management** | Mediates 3–5 arguments before 8am. | Frustrated | Unresolvable without proof of arrival |
| **Clinical work starts** | Begins triage at 8:30am — 2 hours lost to logistics. | Behind, rushed | Lost clinical time never recovered |
| **Blind spots** | Cannot tell administration how many left, how long waits were, where bottlenecks are. | Powerless | No data = no case for resources |

---

## Opportunity Moments

The largest opportunity gaps are:
1. The moment between decision to go and arrival — currently zero product presence
2. The waiting phase — currently zero information provided
3. The abandonment moment — currently invisible to the system
4. The staff's morning setup — currently manual and conflict-prone
